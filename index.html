Game of Throne

🐉 Game of Thrones — Story Overview

Set in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos, Game of Thrones is an epic tale of power, betrayal, loyalty, and survival. Based on the book series A Song of Ice and Fire by George R. R. Martin, the story weaves together multiple plotlines centered around noble families competing for control of the Iron Throne—the seat of ultimate power in the Seven Kingdoms

GOT Houses

House Targaryen

House Targaryen is one of the most important families in Game of Thrones, known for their dragons, silver hair, and claim to the Iron Throne.

House Stark

House Stark is one of the central families in Game of Thrones, ruling the North from Winterfell. They are known for their honor, loyalty, and the warning: “Winter is Coming.”

House Baratheon

House Baratheon is a powerful noble family in Game of Thrones, known for its strength, pride, and claim to the Iron Throne after overthrowing the Targaryens.

House Lannister

House Lannister is one of the richest and most powerful families in Game of Thrones, ruling from the wealthy region of Casterly Rock.
